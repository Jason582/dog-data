@DoggyData

CASE STUDY

Doggy Data is a product that can help you, as a responsible dog owner, make informed choices that will affect your dogs health and wellbeing.

Why another dog app?

It’s true, there is no shortage of dog apps out there, but to find one that is as personalised for you and your dog is another matter.

Don’t get me wrong, there are some wonderful apps on the market, with some of those designs actually inspiring parts of this journey as well.

After alot of thought, however, I was convinced that a better product could be designed that would benefit dogs and dog lovers alike, setting in motion the start of the research process.
